How does it work?

This website was created to help analyze and visualize data related to Lorcana, drawing inspiration from the incredible information and resources available on dreamborn.ink. We are deeply grateful for the tremendous effort that the community puts into growing the Lorcana scene, especially through the creation of fantastic websites.

Our goal with this platform is to contribute by making data visualization simpler and more accessible for everyone. We hope this website can serve as a helpful tool for players, collectors, and fans alike, allowing them to easily explore and understand key data insights from the game. The information on the website is updated weekly to have significant changes and reduce storage costs. Currently, likes or visits are not analyzed. We will improve adding new metrics. If you have any ideas, send them to us!

If you have any questions or suggestions, please feel free to reach out to us at contact@dataplanet.ink.

Thank you for being part of the community, and we look forward to continuing to support Lorcana’s growth together!

Guillermo.
